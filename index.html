Water temperature & override

Use this to tell the fallback model how quickly the water usually warms or cools. Deep lakes lag more; shallow/sheltered water reacts faster.

Onshore can push warmer surface water toward shore; offshore can pull colder water up or away. Leave neutral when you are not sure.

Indoor/heated pools skip the fallback. Outdoor unheated pools use the estimate when no better local reading exists.

If no measured marine water temperature is available, the app can estimate a conservative fallback from recent air temperatures, broad latitude band, season, wind exposure, and water-body type.

Visible only when the toggle is open. Manual water temperature overrides fetched or estimated water data. Quick lookup:seatemperature.infoseatemperature.org

Smart = time-based, terrain/daylight/weather-aware checkpoints with route-aware wind notes. Standard = evenly spaced by route progress.

Start time drives the forecast and daylight. Water temperature uses measured data first, then estimated fallback when possible, with manual override available when you have a better local reading. Loaded routes override preset distance, and route timing overrides duration when available.
